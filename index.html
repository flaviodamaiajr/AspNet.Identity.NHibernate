Welcome to ASP.NET Identity with NHibernate + FluentNHibernate.

I decided to migrate the ASP.NET Identity with Entity Framework to NHibernate with FluentNHibernate when I had to use that Membership system with others relational databases (e.g: MySQL, Oracle, PostgreSQL and others).
